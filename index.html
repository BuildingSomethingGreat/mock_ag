Our Story

Homegrown
Heroes. Home Court.

The Augusta Groove roster features elite talent — the best of the best from our local community and from across the globe. Many are homegrown heroes who advanced from local high schools to successful collegiate careers and professional experience.

Now they're returning home to compete at the highest level in front of the greatest fans in the nation. Partnering with Augusta Sports Council and local businesses — this is professional basketball, right here in Augusta.
